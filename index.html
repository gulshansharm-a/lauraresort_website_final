About Us

Founded by Lawrence D’souza a decade ago, Laura's Resort is a lush 7-acre haven with 350+ mango trees, a diverse organic farm, and a charming mini zoo featuring domestic birds and animals. Our Amusement Park boasts four exciting rides, while the Play Garden and private kids' area offer wholesome family fun. Enjoy aquatic bliss with our four big pools, including one of the town's biggest wave pools, complemented by a vast lawn and a closed hall for events with a 1000-person capacity. For a touch of nature, we provide tree house rooms and dormitory rooms with spacious private galleries.
